TIME TABLE

The Daily Time Table of St. John's Regional Seminary is designed to foster an atmosphere of prayer, study, community life and personal discipline. Every seminarian is expected to observe the schedule faithfully and punctually. The timetable provides a harmonious balance between spiritual formation, academic pursuits, pastoral activities, recreation and rest.
